Assignment brief

Create three Gherkin sequences addressing three different roles approaching the use of a coffee machine

Submission

Scenarios: Coffee Machine - User wants coffee - Staff needs to fill coffee machine with milk - Cleaner needs to clean the machine Feature: New coffee machine A new coffee machine was installed in the office, and staff will use it throughout the day Scenario: Staff member wants a coffee Given: User is in front of the coffee machine And: User has money to pay for coffee When: User enters coffee choice And: User pays for the coffee And: A cup is placed under the spout Then: Coffee is dispensed from the machine as per the user's request And: User's bank account is debited for the cost of the coffee Scenario: Coffee machine has run out of milk Given: Milk tank in the coffee machine is empty When: Building maintenance staff are alerted to the lack of milk And: The milk tank is filled with milk Then: Coffee machine will no longer alert that milk is empty And: Will continue to supply milky coffees to users Scenario: Coffee machine needs cleaning Given: It is the end of the day And: Coffee machine has not already been put through cleaning cycle When: Building maintenance staff put the coffee machine through a cleaning cycle Then: Water and soap is fed through the machine to flush out old coffee And: Coffee machine will no longer alert that a cleaning cycle is required
