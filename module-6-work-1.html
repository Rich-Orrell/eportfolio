Industry 4.0

Submission

Industry 4.0, or the fourth industrial revolution, is defined by the authors as the change in businesses by using new technologies for connectivity, decentralised communication, and access to data.

Remote conferencing is an example which has blown up thanks to remote working practices.

Internet of things is another example (smart objects collecting data to be used for analytical purposes)

Examples of real-world risk

Example 1: Company resources are more accessible to bad actors through the use of social engineering

Example 2: Artificial intelligence tools can be manipulated or mislead
